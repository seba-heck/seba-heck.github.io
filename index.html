From 2020 to 2023 I studied at the ETH Zürich. Although I had to start off in the Corona-time I could visit many great lectures and events. I was especially interested in applied mathematics and more informatic, technical courses. I choose elective courses for Physic-based Simulation, Visual Computing and Computational Physic. This lead me to my specialisation Robotics.

Twice for the course 'System Programming & Computer Architecture' and once for 'Informatik II (D-ITET)' I could take over the role as student teaching assistant. Once a week I could hold an exercise session and help other students to cope with the lecture material.

Besides the lectures I also participated in other events and courses at the ETHZ. A highlight was the PASC Conference. As a volunteer I could look behind the scenes, take part in my first conferences and learn a lot about this field.

In my free time, I often work on different projects, read or get a bit sporty. Then I usually go into the mountains. With my friends and family I go hiking in summer and skiing in winter. I have started to get a pilot's license for paragliding and hope to glide down the mountains in future.
